About


I have always wanted to work in a creative environment, where I can contribute in a production capacity. That and a keen interest in tech lead to a growing interest in Web Design and eventually to General Assembly's Web Development Immersive course.

The course itself was intense and demanding, but above all hugely rewarding. I have learnt so much, through individual and group projects, and yet I have just scratched the surface. That is incredibly exciting.

Through the course, I have have gained skills in both front and back end development and as my career develops, I will ideally be able to progress in both, within a forward-thinking and collaborative team.
